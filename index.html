About Me

Umesh Dangol

I am a NEC-registered Civil and Structural Engineer based in Tokha, Kathmandu. I specialize in structural design, seismic analysis, and construction supervision following Nepal Building Code (NBC 2025) and Indian Standard (IS) codes.

With hands-on experience in residential buildings, commercial structures, and infrastructure projects across Bagmati Province, I bring technical precision and local expertise to every project. I use AutoCAD for design drawings and ETABS for structural analysis to ensure safe and code-compliant structures.
