About Us

Inline Designers & Builders

Inline Designers & Builders is a civil and structural engineering firm based in Tokha, Kathmandu. We specialize in structural design, seismic analysis, and construction supervision following Nepal Building Code (NBC 2025) and Indian Standard (IS) codes.

With hands-on experience in residential buildings, commercial structures, DPR preparation, and infrastructure projects, we bring technical precision and local expertise to every project.
